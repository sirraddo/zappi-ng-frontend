Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

Zappi NG operates the bill payment application at zappi-ng-frontend.vercel.app. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Pi Network identity: Your Pi username and access token to verify identity.
  • Social login: Public profile name and email from Google, Facebook, or Twitter.
  • Transaction data: Records of bill payments and Pi transfers you initiate.
  • Device info: Browser type and IP address for security.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate your identity and provide app access
  • To process bill payments and Pi Network transactions
  • To maintain your transaction history
  • To improve our services and comply with Nigerian law

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with Pi Network (for auth), Nigerian utility providers (for bill payment), and when required by law.

4. Data Security

All data is encrypted in transit (TLS/HTTPS). We retain your data as long as your account is active or as required by law.

5. Your Rights

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

6. Data Deletion

To delete your data, email support@zappi.ng with subject "Data Deletion Request". We will process within 30 days. You can also visit our data deletion page.

7. Contact

Email: support@zappi.ng · Website: zappi-ng-frontend.vercel.app
